About CHROMA

CHROMA is a revolutionary bioinformatics and design pipeline that assigns all known human genes a "colour profile". We believe that every human gene has a number of features that can be mapped to colour theory principles ï¿½. These principles have guided the development of brands, logos, and colour palettes for more than 50 years. We believe it is time for colour theory to help researchers gain new insights into all of the genes in the human genome.

Here is a brief overview of our 3-step pipeline:

  1. We assemble a list of all known genes in the human genome from Ensembl
  2. We annotate each gene by integrating data from a variety of bioinformatics sources, including ChEMBL, UniProt, and Open Targets
  3. We map all gene annotations and features to colour theory principles

To learn more about the pipeline, read our paper in Nature Designs or check out our code on GitHub.
