Move over Pantone Colour Of The Year!

Target Of The Year is a revolutionary bioinformatics and design pipeline that assigns all known genes a "colour profile" using colour theory. We believe that colour theory can help researchers find new therapeutic drug targets because each target has various attributes that we have mapped to established colour theory principles. These principles have guided the development of brands, logos, and colour palettes for more than 50 years - and now, we believe it's time for colour theory to guide the development of new therapeutic drugs .

Our pipeline:

  • Assembles a list of all known genes in the human genome
  • Assesses each gene's attributes using our pipeline
  • Integrates data from a variety of bioinformatics sources, including ChEMBL, DrugBank, Ensembl, UniProt, and Open Targets
  • Provides users with information about each target along with relevant external links for further analysis

To learn more about the pipeline, read our paper in Nature Design or check out our code on GitHub.
